The Artist

Rooted in Tradition

Born from the rich cultural heritage of Mozambique, my journey as a sculptor began with the ancient hardwoods of my homeland. Each piece carries the spirit of African craftsmanship, honoring traditions passed down through generations.

Contemporary Vision

While deeply rooted in tradition, my work embraces contemporary forms and concepts. I transform raw Mozambican hardwood into sculptures that bridge the gap between ancestral wisdom and modern artistic expression.
